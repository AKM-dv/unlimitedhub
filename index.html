Use Case & Wallet Type

Details requested by Razorpay covering our business model, wallet classification, and live functionality.

Core Use Case

UnlimitedHearts is a relationship-focused marketplace that lets adults discover verified creators, talk over secure voice/video, and redeem earned rewards inside the in-app shop.

  • Users recharge via Razorpay and consume Minutes (voice) or Diamonds (video).
  • Creators earn INR credits that can be spent on curated products.
  • Referral system expands the community organically and rewards both sides.
Closed Wallet

Wallet Classification

Our Smart Wallet is closed-loop under RBI guidelines:

  • Funds are issued only after a Razorpay payment succeeds.
  • Balances stay within UnlimitedHearts and can be used solely for our digital minutes, diamonds, and curated commerce catalog.
  • No cash withdrawal, P2P transfer, or third-party merchant payouts are possible.

Current Functional State

  • Android build is in closed beta via direct APK download.
  • Admin console manages onboarding, KYC, pricing, and payouts.
  • User app already handles login, wallet recharge, voice/video calling, and order tracking.

Public launches to Google Play & App Store are scheduled once the payment gateway is approved.

Payment & Wallet Flow

Step-by-step payment journey requested by Razorpay.

01

User Onboarding

Users complete OTP login and gender verification before any wallet action. Admin panel can pause or block users instantly.

02

Recharge via Razorpay

Razorpay Checkout is used for all top-ups (UPI, cards, netbanking). Successful payments credit INR to the closed wallet ledger.

03

Auto Conversion

INR gets auto-mapped to Minutes (voice) or Diamonds (video) at preset exchange rates; no manual intervention required.

04

Service Consumption

Calls decrement balances in real time. Females simultaneously earn INR credits inside the Earnings wallet.

05

In-App Redemption

Earnings can only purchase items inside the UnlimitedHearts shop. Any additional amount is collected again through Razorpay.

Compliance Snapshot

  • Wallet Type: Closed wallet (issuer = UnlimitedHearts).
  • Use Case: Digital content, virtual calling minutes, and curated commerce.
  • Settlement: Razorpay → UnlimitedHearts nodal → internal ledger.
  • No P2P, remittance, or cash-out pathways exist.
